As this project originated as a capstone project for the team as students in Arizona State University's School of Earth
and Space Exploration we wanted to include some of the relevant files from the capstone class. This folder contains the
milestone presentations as well as the final report of our team's capstone, highlighting the changes that were made to
our project throughout the year. During our first semester, we built the SRR, PDR, and CDR. By the end of our second
semester, we developed our Final Presentation and Final Report, while carrying out the rest of the project. These files
may contain errors that were later corrected, or information and ideas that changed as the project went on, they are
here for the purpose of illustrating our teams's thought and work processes.
