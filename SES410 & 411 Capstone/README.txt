As this project originated as a capstone project for the team as students in Arizona State University's School of Earth
and Space Exploration we wanted to include some of the relevant files from the capstone class. This folder contains the
milestone presentations as well as the final report of our team's capstone, highlighting the changes that were made to
our project throughout the year. During the first semester (Fall 2025), we built the SRR, PDR, and CDR. By the end of the
second semester (Spring 2026), we developed our Final Presentation and Final Report, while carrying out the project itself. 

These files may contain errors that were later corrected, or information and ideas that changed as the project went on,
they are here for the purpose of illustrating our teams's thought and work processes. Additionally, as these presentations
are saved as .pdfs, any animations will not work, generally, any slides after the one(s) saying thank you and/or asking
for questions are extra slides that may not have been used during the presentation, but were left in in case they were
needed for answering audience questions.
